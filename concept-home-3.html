Our approach

We start with your community.

Before we build anything, we listen. We talk to your customers, your neighbors, your regulars. We find out what they actually say about you when you're not in the room — and we build your marketing around that truth.

We capture and tell their stories.

Real video. Real voices. Real moments. Our content team documents the authentic relationships between your business and your community. Not testimonials — stories. There's a difference, and audiences feel it immediately.

AI finds more of them.

Once we know who your audience is and what resonates with them, we use AI to find thousands more people exactly like them. Across Google, social platforms, and AI search engines. Precision at scale — without losing the human signal that started it.

Top Drone School

Train. Certify. Get Paid.

Top Drone School teaches students to execute single-take, high-speed cinematic drone tours called "One Shots" — uncut, continuous FPV drone flights that capture a complete location story. No edits. No spliced clips. Just a single, perfectly executed run at up to 97 kph.

The Four-Step Path

01

Get Certified

FAA Part 107 Remote Pilot Certification — regulations, airspace, weather, and a full mock exam.

02

Know Your Gear

Master the DJI Avata 2 — FPV Remote Controller 3, Goggles 3, Manual Mode, ND filters, and imaging workflow.

03

Complete the Course

Four-phase indoor FPV training course simulating building and tight-space navigation.

04

Get Paid

Earn $25/hour capturing one-shot business video tours for local clients.

Today, with AI reshaping how people find and trust information, we help clients lead in that shift — not get left behind by it.
